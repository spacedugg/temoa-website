## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do you measure success?**
Multiple metrics: keyword ranking positions, organic sales growth, conversion rate improvements, traffic increases, reduced return rates. Success = more visibility, more traffic, more conversions, more profit.

**How long does Amazon listing optimization take?**
Initial optimization: 1-2 weeks for research, content creation, and implementation. Results: 30-60 days to see full impact as Amazon indexes changes and rankings improve.

**Will optimizing my listing hurt my current rankings?**
Not when done correctly. We use gradual rollout strategy, making changes systematically and monitoring impact. We never make drastic changes that could trigger ranking drops.

**How often should I optimize my Amazon listing?**
Quarterly refreshes recommended for most products. More frequent for seasonal products or highly competitive categories. The marketplace evolves—your listings should too.

We provide complete photography services—in our studio or on-location. We handle everything from concept to final deliverables. You can also use existing photography if it meets quality standards.

**Can you guarantee first-page rankings?**
No honest agency can guarantee specific rankings—too many variables. However, we do guarantee improvement and have a track record averaging 340% increase in first-page keywords.

**What if my product is in a very competitive niche?**
Competitive niches require sophisticated strategies—long-tail keywords, superior creative, strategic A+ Content, and strong review profiles. We've succeeded in highly competitive categories through comprehensive optimization.

**Do you optimize international listings?**
Yes. We optimize listings for UK, EU, Canada, Mexico, and other marketplaces. Each requires localization—not just translation, but cultural adaptation.
