## Amazon DSP: Programmatic Advertising That Drives Results

Amazon DSP (Demand-Side Platform) lets businesses promote products and services across Amazon-owned sites/apps and third-party exchanges through display, audio, and video ads purchased programmatically.

**Amazon-Owned** – Amazon.com, Prime Video, Twitch, IMDb, Amazon Music, Fire TV, Kindle

**Third-Party Publishers** – Premium websites, news sites, entertainment platforms, mobile apps

**Audio Inventory** – Streaming music, podcasts (iHeartMedia's 870+ stations), broadcast radio

Full-funnel advertising · On & off Amazon · Advanced audience targeting · Streaming TV

### How It's Different from Sponsored Ads

Regular Amazon PPC only reaches shoppers actively searching on Amazon. DSP reaches people everywhere, before they get to Amazon. Think of PPC as capturing existing demand, DSP as creating and building demand. DSP executes full-funnel campaigns—targeting customers at awareness, consideration, purchase, and post-purchase loyalty stages using different targeting strategies.
