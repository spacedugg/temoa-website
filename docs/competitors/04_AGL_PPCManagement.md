Typical range: 5-20% of revenue. Depends on category, competition, margins, and stage. New products may require 30-40% initially. We provide specific recommendations during your free audit.

**What is a good ACoS for Amazon PPC?**
No universal answer—depends on margins and goals. Category averages: 15-35%. We focus on TACoS accounting for organic sales. Your target should align with specific margins and objectives.

**How long does it take to see results from Amazon PPC services?**
Initial improvements: 30-45 days. Significant results: 60-90 days (major ACoS reductions, revenue growth). Continuous optimization ongoing. Best performers at 12 months.
